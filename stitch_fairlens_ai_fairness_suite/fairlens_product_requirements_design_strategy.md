# FairLens: AI Bias Detection & Fairness Audit Platform

## Core Design Principles
- **Cinematic Onboarding**: Emotional, interactive, and memorable.
- **Enterprise SaaS**: Structured, trustworthy, and scalable.
- **Micro-interactions**: 200-400ms smooth transitions, animating numbers, hover glows.
- **Particle System**: Subtle background data signals that change color based on bias (Gray -> Red -> Green).

## Onboarding Flow
1. **Screen 1: Hook** - "Your AI has made 10,000 decisions. Were they fair?"
2. **Screen 2: Upload** - Drag-and-drop dataset analysis.
3. **Screen 3: Analysis** - Neural pulse animation.
4. **Screen 4: Suspicion** - Progressive text revealing disparate impact.
5. **Screen 5: Name-Swap Demo** - The core moment where changing "Sarah Chen" to "James White" spikes the score.
6. **Screen 6: Shock** - Animated count of unfairly rejected candidates (3,241).

## Product UI
- **Main Dashboard**: High-level Fairness Score and Risk Cards.
- **Bias Insights**: Statement-based analysis over heavy charts.
- **Investigation Mode**: Real-time attribute swapping and score updates.
- **Remediation**: Before/After toggle for bias fixes.
- **Reports**: Clean, professional export views.
